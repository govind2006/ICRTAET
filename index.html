About ICRTAET 2019
 
 
ICRTAET 2019 is the sixth conference of its name being organized by Shri Mata Vaishno Devi University, Katra during its technical fest TITIKSHA. The theme of the conference is based on the recent developments in the field of engineering and technology with an aim to provide a common platform to researchers, students, academicians and industrialists from different fields to present their work and indulge in a constructive discussions to further improve their research.



About University
 
 
Akin to Brahma (God), who fosters and nurtures the Universe, science over the years has bestowed upon man god like powers, there by making him the dominant species on globe Vigyanam Brahma or God is Science is the motto Shri Mata Vaishno Devi University has been established through an act of J&K Legislature as an autonomous, fully residential and highly technical University of excellence with grant and continuing support from Shri Mata Vaishno Devi Shrine Board, Katra. Govt., of India has supported our projects and the industry has been closely involved in our R&D activities.The University has the unique distinction of being inaugurated by the President of India in the year 2004 and the first convocation addressed by the Prime Minister of India in the year 2008. The University is also making an effort to create an extensive university-industry cooperation for economic growth of the area as well as the rest of the country. There are many on going research projects with assistance from many supporting institutions.

Abstract Format
 
Each submitted abstract must include title, author’s name, author’s affiliation, author’s e-mail address at the top and include 5 to 8 keywords. Abstract must be greater than 200 words but less than 500 words. Authors of selected abstracts will be asked to prepare final proof-read camera ready version of their abstracts and must include the modification/suggestions of the reviewer and send them before the indicated deadline. Detailed instructions and template will be provided separately
Abstract Submission
 
Submitted abstracts should be original, state the purpose and significance of the study and contain contributions of theoretical, experimental, review or application nature. Authors can submit their abstracts by sending it via email to

icrtaet@gmail.com
Full Paper Submission
 
Authors of selected abstracts will be asked to submit their full-length manuscript of minimum length of 4 pages and maximum of 6 pages in IEEE A4 page size double-column format. All full length manuscripts will be peer-reviewed and published in international journals of high indexing.

*Full Paper Submission is not mandatory

BEST PAPER AWARD
 
Top 3 papers presented in ICRTAET 2019 will be acknowledged with best paper awards and certificates.
